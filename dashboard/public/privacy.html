1. Information We Collect

Velync collects information to provide and improve our integration platform. When you use our service, we collect:

Account Information

When you sign up, we collect your email address, display name, and authentication credentials (handled securely via Firebase Authentication).

Integration Data

To perform syncs between your connected platforms, we temporarily access and process data through the OAuth tokens you authorize. This includes reading, writing, and syncing records such as tasks, contacts, notes, or other entities you choose to integrate. We do not store the full content of your records beyond what is necessary to perform the sync.

OAuth Credentials

When you connect a third-party platform (e.g., Google Contacts, TickTick, Notion), we store encrypted OAuth tokens to maintain the connection. These tokens are stored securely in Firestore and are used solely to facilitate the integrations you configure.

Usage Data

We use Google Analytics to collect usage statistics such as page views, sign-up and login events, sync frequency, and feature usage, to help us understand and improve the platform. Google Analytics assigns your browser a unique identifier via cookies — see Section 8 (Cookies) for details.

AI-Assisted Features

When you use our field-mapping suggestion feature, the structure of your source and destination fields (such as field names and types) is sent to Google's Gemini model, via Google Cloud Vertex AI, to generate mapping suggestions. We only send the field schema information needed to suggest a mapping — we do not knowingly send the full content of your records to the AI model.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Velync platform
  • Execute the sync configurations you create between your connected platforms
  • Provide AI-assisted field-mapping suggestions using Google's Gemini model (via Vertex AI)
  • Send service-related communications (e.g., sync failure alerts, policy updates), delivered via our backend infrastructure on Google Cloud
  • Provide customer support and troubleshoot issues
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the operation of the integration service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent — When you authorize a sync between platforms, data flows directly between those platforms according to your configuration.
  • Service providers — We rely on the following providers to operate Velync, each processing data only as needed to provide their service:
    • Firebase / Google Cloud Platform — hosting, database, and authentication infrastructure.
    • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing for paid subscriptions. Lemon Squeezy receives your payment details directly; we never see or store your full card number.
    • Google Vertex AI (Gemini) — AI-assisted field-mapping suggestions (see Section 1).
    • Google reCAPTCHA / Firebase App Check — bot and abuse prevention.
    • Google Analytics — usage analytics (see Section 1 and Section 8).
  • Legal requirements — If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account data — Retained until you delete your account.
  • Sync configurations — Retained until deleted by you or when your account is deleted.
  • Execution logs — Retained according to your plan's log-retention setting (7 days by default on the Free plan; longer on paid plans), for debugging and monitoring purposes.
  • OAuth tokens — Retained until you revoke the connection or delete your account.

You can delete your workspace or your entire account at any time from within the app (Settings → Account → Delete My Account, or Workspace Settings → Delete Workspace). Deletion is immediate and self-service — it cascades to your sync configurations, field mappings, connected accounts, stored credentials, and execution logs. You do not need to contact us to do this, though you're welcome to if you have questions.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted via TLS 1.3 encryption
  • OAuth tokens and credentials are encrypted at rest and stored in a database collection with security rules that deny all direct client-side read or write access — they are only ever accessible through our own server-side infrastructure
  • Authentication handled by Firebase Auth (Google Cloud)
  • Regular security audits and dependency updates
  • Minimal data access principle — we only access data required to perform the sync

6. Third-Party Services

Velync integrates with third-party platforms you explicitly authorize, and relies on other third parties to operate the service itself. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data:

  • Google — covers Google Cloud Platform / Firebase (hosting, database, authentication), Google Analytics (usage analytics), Google reCAPTCHA / Firebase App Check (bot prevention), and Google Vertex AI / Gemini (AI-assisted field-mapping suggestions) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing for paid subscriptions — Lemon Squeezy Privacy Policy
  • TickTick — connected as an integration platform you authorize — TickTick Privacy Policy
  • Notion — connected as an integration platform you authorize — Notion Privacy Policy
  • WhatsApp (Meta) — if you use the in-app WhatsApp support button, your conversation takes place on WhatsApp, governed by Meta's policies — WhatsApp Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. You should review their policies, particularly for platforms you choose to connect to Velync (TickTick, Notion, and any others).

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to data portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent — Revoke OAuth tokens at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Cookies

Velync uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication — Firebase Authentication uses session cookies to maintain your login state.
  • Analytics — We use Google Analytics, which sets cookies (such as _ga) to help us understand how the platform is used. This is covered in more detail in Section 1.
  • Security — Google reCAPTCHA / Firebase App Check may set cookies as part of verifying that requests come from a real browser rather than a bot.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, and we do not sell or share cookie data for advertising purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or through the platform. Continued use of Velync after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
